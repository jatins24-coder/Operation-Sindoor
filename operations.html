Other Indian Military Operations

Major Operations Against Pakistan

  • Operation Meghdoot (1984): Capture of Siachen Glacier.
  • Operation Trident (1971): Naval attack on Karachi harbor.
  • Operation Safed Sagar (1999): Air operations during the Kargil War.
  • Balakot Airstrike (2019): Airstrike on terror camps post-Pulwama attack.
  • Operation Dwarka (1965): Naval bombardment of Dwarka.
