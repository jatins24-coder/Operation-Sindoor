Why Operation Sindoor?

Background

Operation Sindoor was launched in response to a brutal attack in Indian-administered Kashmir that claimed the lives of more than 20 civilians. This provocation was linked to cross-border terrorism supported by elements based in Pakistan.

Objectives

  • Neutralize terror launchpads in Pakistan-occupied regions.
  • Dismantle communication and logistics hubs supporting terror groups.
  • Send a strategic message of deterrence to hostile forces.
