How Was Operation Sindoor Executed?

Tactics and Execution

The Indian Armed Forces executed a swift multi-branch attack using fighter jets, long-range artillery, and special forces. Targets included militant camps and logistics hubs deep inside enemy territory.

Key Developments

  • Destruction of multiple terror bases.
  • Neutralization of Abdul Rauf Azhar, a top Jaish-e-Mohammed commander.
  • Use of satellite-guided missiles and precision airstrikes.
